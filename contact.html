Details to prepare

Customers should have these details ready before requesting party rentals.

  • Event date and preferred rental time window.
  • Event city and setup address area.
  • Estimated guest count and age range.
  • Rental items needed, such as bounce house, tables, chairs, or canopy.
  • Setup surface, available space, gate access, and parking notes.
